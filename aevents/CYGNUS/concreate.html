OBJECTIVES

To prepare Concrete Mix Design.
To use efficiently Eco-friendly materials such as Fly ash, etc (optional).

NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS PER GROUP: 2-4
INSTRUCTION / RULES:
1. At the day of event concrete cube should have age of 7 days.
2. The concrete should be made with natural or artificial ingredients &using any Eco-friendly material or/and admixture without reinforcement or fibres.
3. For Concrete Mix Design you can adopt any method like IS, DOE or combination of other methods.
4. For cubes you can use only conventional method of curing.

RULES

5. Casting of cubes & Curing should be done in Concrete Technology laboratory of Participants College only.
6. Steel fibers are not allowed to be used as admixture while the other admixture is permitted.
7. Participants must bring design sheet at the time of cube testing signed by the Head of Department & the Faculty concerned along with the date and Venue of casting of cubes and the method of curing.
8. In Mix design sheet, details of step by step calculations are required with clear mention of calculations of any other materials used. The sheet should be signed by HOD and the concerned staff.
9. The problem statement will be displayed, 8-9 days before the date of event on the website of Perception.

RULES

10. Participants should contact to event in-charge for confirmation of date for problem displayed.
11. The grade of concrete will be given in the problem statement.
12. The given strength is ≥ strength after 7(seven) days.
Judging criteria:* • Standard deviations with respect to given strength in problem.
• 1) Mean strength ≥ strength is as given in the problem.
• 2) Mean strength as close to strength given in the problem.
• 3) Standard deviation as low as possible. *The authority of decisions will remain with the judges and the decisions taken by Judges will be final and binding.
