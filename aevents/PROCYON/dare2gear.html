OBJECTIVES

The track with sharp and regular turns, ascending and descending slopes, and Build a wireless, remote controlled machine, powered only by an “Internal Combustion Engine”, to complete many more
MACHINE SPECIFICATIONS:
Machine should fit in a box of dimensions 700mm X 500mm X 500mm at any moment of time during the race. The external device which is used to control the machine is not included in the size constraint. The machine should be controlled by a wireless remote control mechanism throughout the race.

Participants Per Team:5 (Students from different educational institutes can form a team)

1. The internal combustion engine capacity can’t exceed 4 cc.
2. The track length to be completed is about 100 m. Hence participants are required to have sufficient fuel tank capacity.
3. Participants are required to bring the fuel with them, Fuel will not be provided by the organizers.
4. The name of your machine must be prominently displayed on the machine.
5. The teams have to obtain the practice session slots at the time of reporting. The slots will be given on a first come first served basis.
6. Judges reserve the right to disqualify any team for their misbehaviour.
7. Multiple entries are allowed.
8. Damage to the track will lead to disqualification of the team.
9. The changes will be notified to participants and will be highlighted on the website.
10. In any case, Judges Decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.
11. In each round of competition, at a time, only one team will be performing on the track.

12. To avoid the frequency clashes, every participating team has to submit their remote with organizing committee, when other team is performing. The team failing to do so will be immediately disqualified.
13. In first round, each team will be given two chances to complete the track.
14. On the basis of least time required to complete the track, teams will be selected for next round.
15. The track will have certain no. of check points. If at any point, a machine halts, or goes off the arena, it has to start from the checkpoint, previously covered.
16. Team members are not permitted to touch their machines once the race begins(except hand touches in case of tumbling or halting). The penalty for doing so is, disqualification.
17. The organizing committee reserves the rights to change the rules at any time.
