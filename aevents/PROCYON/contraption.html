OBJECTIVES

Take the energy of your brains to its peak by designing a contraption. The rules are simple..!
EVENT DETAILS:
Participant must submit their abstracts before the event.

Participants per team : 6(max)

RULES

1 The contraption should have a minimum of 8 steps.
2 The maximum time limit is 30 minutes.
3 Points are based on steps and energy conversions used.
4 Each team can have a maximum of 6 members.
5 Only 2 trials are allowed. No points will be deducted for any step in the second trial. However,if a team takes the second trial, then 85% of the total points scored in the second trial will be considered or 100% of the total points for the completed steps of the first trial will be considered whichever is higher. However, if the team does not take the second trial, 100% of the total points of the first trial will be taken. 6 Parallel contraption would carry 1.5 times the points as the main one. The longer of the 2 parallel contraptions will be considered as the main one.
7 Maximum of 3 interventions (stoppage in the process) are allowed in any trial. For each intervention exceeding the three permissible, 5 points will be deducted.

ROUND 1: 1 Participants are required to submit an abstract of their contraption. Points will be allotted for it.
ABSTRACT DETAILS:
1 Details of each and every participating member (name, phone no., college, class).
2 Number of energy conversions.
3 Sketches, flowcharts explaining steps.
4 Explanation of each and every step.
5 Explanation must be clear and simple.
ROUND 2:
Selection for the second round is based on the points allotted in the first round. The contraption is to be carried out in a space of 6ft * 6ft (maximum). The participants are requested to bring copies of properly printed write ups of their contraption, clearly mentioning the steps and number of energy conversions. Participants will be provided with a mass of 100 grams. Participants are also allowed to get their own mass which will be checked before the event.
